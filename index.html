About Us

Unlike all the other servers out there, NemoXN isn't just a Minecraft server filled with plugins, shops, and pointless economy gimmicks that ruin the game. We provide a fun semi-modded experience while keeping the game competitive and grindy. NemoXN has multiple seasons that feature new plugins and a different yet fun experience. As a server that values fairness, we do not tolerate anything pay-to-win, however we are always open to player suggestions and donations. Our community is very respectful and non-toxic, something other servers cannot provide. Our staff team mirors these aspects, as they are very supportive and easy-going. Join NemoXN today and have fun playing on a one-of-a-kind, amazing server, unlike anything you've seen before!
